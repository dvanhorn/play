Glycerin, Soy Lecithin, Witch Hazel, Rosewater, Grapeseed Oil Infusion

Allspice, Star Anise)

\noindent
{\bf To Use:} Wet hands or body with warm water and gently massage
into skin. Rinse and pat dry. Avoid contact with face, eyes and broken
skin.
